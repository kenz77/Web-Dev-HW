About Me

The materials I use for this instructable are the easiest materials I have used when making pinch pots. This is good for beginners who are not very familiar with working with clay.

Even if you are not an art major or have an art studio of your own, this is a fun and easy project that you can do in your own home. I highly recommend this activity to be added to your to-do list.

This is just a small, simple project that I have done. I am also into making oil painting, drawing with charcoal, and printmaking. I have also done more advanced pottery such as making a 6 foot pot out of red clay and I am currently working on wheele throwing which is really hard at first, but once you get the hang of it is a lot of fun. This is an art worth learning.
