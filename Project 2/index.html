How To Make A Clay Pot

By: Mackenzie Lightfield

In this guide, you will learn how to make a simple pot out of clay.
By the end you with know the basics and be able to use more creativity
for your next pot.

These Are The Items You Will Need:

    red clay
    bucket
    water
    fork
    needle tool

Everyone Should Learn To Make Clay Creations!

This will teach you how to use both your hands and simple tools to create a
work of art that will look great. You can keep these in your home, give
them as gifts, or even sell them in a craft fair. Creating pottery is a skill
that is both useful as well as fun. Proceed to Instructions
