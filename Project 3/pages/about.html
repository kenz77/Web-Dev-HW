About Shel Silverstein

Shel Silverstein is a man of many talents. He has writen several poetry books for children which many adore and love. Silverstein also sings, writes his own songs and plays guitar. Drawing cartoons is also one of his hobbies/talents. His drawings show up in all his books because he likes to use his own illustrations.



Fun Fact!

Since Shel Siverstein was also a talented song writer and musician, he wrote over 800 songs and released 9 original albums in his lifetime.
